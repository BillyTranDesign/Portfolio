The Brief



Katani Orlando hosts unique anime-themed boba conventions, where boba tea concoctions are inspired by characters and elements from popular anime series. I collaborated with Katani Orlando to design merchandise and cupsleeves, establishing their brandmark and fundamental cupsleeves.

Additionally, I spearheaded Instagram as a digital marketing strategy, helping the brand quickly grow by 3,500 followers through four events locally hosted in Orlando, Florida. Katani Orlando continues to thrive, hosting events with exclusive merchandise tailored to each theme and supporting local vendors who offer crafts related to the event. Katani strives to host these events seasonally throughout the year. I look forward to their future collaborations!
