The Brief



Working with clientele Dr. Loo, an expert in Alternative Medicine and CEO of Dr. Loo's Natural Remedies based in San Jose, California, it was an honor to help shape and create colorful graphics, cohesive labels, and provide digital marketing experiences.

By providing exceptional product design that is safe and ergonomic, these Harmony Patches are made with natural and organic ingredients to soothe tummy aches and coughs of all ages. Dr. Loo's holistic healing patches are water-based. The branding design reflects the all-natural ingredients and includes elements such as leafy patterns and soft pastels to match the "gentle" healing nature of Dr. Loo's products.
