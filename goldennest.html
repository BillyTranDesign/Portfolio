The Brief



Golden Nest is a premium brand specializing in authentic bird’s nest and Asian delicacies. I collaborated with Golden Nest to design engaging email campaigns that promoted exclusive offers, loyalty rewards, and seasonal sales. Each campaign blended clean layouts with culturally resonant visuals, balancing modern e-commerce best practices with Golden Nest’s heritage-driven branding.

I designed the campaigns in Figma, where I developed modular layouts and reusable components to streamline future iterations. Once approved, I deployed the graphics onto Klaviyo, ensuring responsive formatting and a smooth integration with Golden Nest’s marketing workflows. By pairing strong visual hierarchy with clear calls-to-action, these campaigns boosted engagement and supported customer retention. Golden Nest continues to leverage these designs in their ongoing digital strategy, strengthening their connection with a loyal customer base.
