Oh Hey there!

I’m Billy a graphic & packaging designer with 6+ years of experience (yes, I’ve officially spent more time with boxes than most people do with their plants). Based in the buzzing Bay Area, California, I blend my love for design with the energy of this vibrant community!

My goal? To create designs that don’t just look good but actually speak to people kind of like that one friend who always tells the best stories (but with less coffee spilled). Over the years, I’ve worked on everything from product packaging to branding, always aiming for that sweet spot where function meets eye candy.

When I’m not designing, you’ll probably find me trying to cook up new recipes, or heading out on spontaneous adventures; because who doesn’t need a little inspiration (and some good snacks) in their life? Oh, and I’m a content creator too, sharing my journey and connecting with other creatives who understand that sometimes design is 90% caffeine and 10% magic.


Let’s connect and and let the good times roll!
